<p><strong>The Car Diffuser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Car Diffuser</li><li>Electric Heat Car Diffuser</li><li>Evaporated Car Diffuser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car diffusers come in three main types: Ultrasonic car diffusers, electric heat car diffusers, and evaporated car diffusers. Ultrasonic car diffusers use ultrasonic vibrations to disperse essential oils into the air. Electric heat car diffusers use a heating element to evaporate the essential oils. Evaporated car diffusers typically use a pad or filter to absorb and slowly release the essential oils into the air. Each type of diffuser offers unique benefits and features to enhance the driving experience.</p></p>
