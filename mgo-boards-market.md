<p><ul><li>Thin (Thickness Below 8 mm)</li><li>Medium (Thickness 8-15 mm)</li><li>Thick (Thickness Above 15mm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MgO boards are classified into three main types based on their thickness: Thin (below 8 mm), Medium (8-15 mm), and Thick (above 15 mm). Thin boards are commonly used in applications where weight and space are a concern, such as in mobile homes or lightweight partitions. Medium boards are versatile and suitable for various interior and exterior construction projects. Thick boards are ideal for heavy-duty applications that require durability and strength, such as in flooring or structural support.</p></p>
