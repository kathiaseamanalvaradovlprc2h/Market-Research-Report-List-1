<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Pharmaceutical Inks</li><li>Solvent-based Pharmaceutical Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical inks are used for printing information on pharmaceutical packaging. Water-based pharmaceutical inks are made with water as the main solvent, making them less toxic and more environmentally friendly. Solvent-based pharmaceutical inks use organic solvents that evaporate during the drying process. Water-based inks are preferred for their safety and sustainability, while solvent-based inks are known for their durability and resistance to water and chemicals. Both types of inks are essential for ensuring accurate labeling and information on pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
