<p><p>Automotive Headlight Light Distribution Control System (AHB) is a technology that adjusts the distribution of light from headlights based on driving conditions. The two main types are Adaptive Front-lighting System, which swivels headlights in the direction of the vehicle's path, and Adaptive Driving Beam System, which automatically adjusts the light pattern to avoid glaring other drivers while maintaining optimal visibility. Both systems enhance driver visibility and safety by providing better lighting in various driving situations.</p></p>
