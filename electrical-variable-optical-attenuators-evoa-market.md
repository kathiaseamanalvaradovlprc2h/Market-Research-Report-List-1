<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>4 Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrical Variable Optical Attenuators (EVOA) market consists of two main types - Single Channel and 4 Channel attenuators. Single Channel attenuators are designed to adjust the intensity of a single optical signal, providing precise control over the signal strength. On the other hand, 4 Channel attenuators are capable of simultaneously controlling the intensity of four different optical signals, making them ideal for applications requiring adjustment of multiple channels. Both types of attenuators offer flexibility and reliability in managing optical signals in various telecommunications and networking systems.</p></p>
