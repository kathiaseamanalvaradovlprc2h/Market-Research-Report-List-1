<p><p>Metal casting robots market can be divided into two main types: Semi-Automatic and Fully Automatic. Semi-Automatic robots require some human intervention for loading and unloading the molds, while the robots handle the rest of the casting process. Fully Automatic robots, on the other hand, are capable of completing the entire metal casting process without any human intervention. These robots are equipped with advanced technology such as sensors and cameras to ensure precise and efficient casting operations. Both types of robots are widely used in industries for their ability to enhance productivity and safety.</p></p>
