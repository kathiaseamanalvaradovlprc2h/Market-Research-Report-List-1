OCT scanners are used for non-invasive imaging of the retina. Fundus cameras capture detailed images of the back of the eye. Visual field analyzers measure central and peripheral vision. Ophthalmoscopes are used for examining the inside of the eye. Retinoscopes are used to determine the refractive error of the eye. These devices play a critical role in diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions.</p></p>
