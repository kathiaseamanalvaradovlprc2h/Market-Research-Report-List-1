<p><p>Cleanroom luminaires are specialized lighting fixtures used in cleanroom environments to ensure proper lighting conditions while maintaining clean and sterile conditions. There are two types of cleanroom luminaires: recessed luminaires and surface-mounted luminaires. Recessed luminaires are installed flush into the ceiling, providing a seamless and unobtrusive lighting solution. Surface-mounted luminaires, on the other hand, are attached directly onto the surface of the ceiling, offering a more visible lighting option. Both types are designed to meet strict cleanroom requirements and provide efficient and reliable lighting for various applications.</p></p>
