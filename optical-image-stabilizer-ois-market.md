<p><strong>The Optical Image Stabilizer (OIS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lens Shift Method</li><li>Module Tilt Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Image Stabilization (OIS) technology in cameras and smartphones includes two main types: Lens Shift Method and Module Tilt Method. The Lens Shift Method stabilizes images by moving the lens elements to counteract any unwanted movements. On the other hand, the Module Tilt Method stabilizes images by tilting the whole image sensor module. Both methods aim to reduce blurriness and improve image quality by compensating for shaky hands or movements while capturing a photo or video.</p></p>
