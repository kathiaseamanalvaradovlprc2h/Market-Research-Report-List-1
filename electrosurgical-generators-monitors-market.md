<p><p>Electrosurgical Generators Monitors Market consists of three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Generator, Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator, and Vessel Sealing Generator. Monopole generators deliver electrical energy through a single active electrode, while Bipolar generators use two active electrodes to minimize tissue damage. Vessel Sealing generators utilize a combination of pressure and energy to seal blood vessels during surgery. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different surgical applications based on the specific needs of the procedure.</p></p>
