<p><p>Basalt reinforced fiber is a type of fiber derived from basalt rock. There are two main types of basalt reinforced fiber markets: Basalt Chopped Fiber and Basalt Continuous Fiber. Basalt chopped fiber consists of short lengths of basalt fibers that are typically used in applications such as concrete reinforcement. Basalt continuous fiber, on the other hand, consists of continuous strands of basalt fibers that offer high strength and durability, making them suitable for use in various industries such as aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
