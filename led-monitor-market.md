<p><strong>The LED Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Lit LED Monitor</li><li>Edge Lit LED monitor</li><li>Full Array LED Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Monitor market types include Direct Lit, Edge Lit, and Full Array monitors. Direct Lit monitors have LED backlights placed directly behind the screen for a uniform brightness across the display. Edge Lit monitors have LED backlights placed around the edges of the screen, resulting in a thinner profile and lower power consumption. Full Array monitors have LED backlights covering the entire display for precise brightness control and high picture quality. Each type offers different advantages in terms of lighting technology and display performance.</p></p>
