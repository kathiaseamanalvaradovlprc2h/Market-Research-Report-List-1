<p><p>Dolby Atmos soundbars come in two main market types: Upward-firing soundbars and Virtualized soundbars. Upward-firing soundbars use speakers that project sound upward, bouncing it off the ceiling to create an immersive 3D audio experience. Virtualized soundbars, on the other hand, simulate the effect of Dolby Atmos without the need for upward-firing speakers, using advanced audio processing technology to create a surround sound effect. Both market types offer consumers a range of options for experiencing high-quality Dolby Atmos audio in their homes.</p></p>
