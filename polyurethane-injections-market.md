The market is divided into three main types based on viscosity: low viscosity, very low viscosity, and other. Low viscosity injections are suitable for fine cracks and voids, while very low viscosity injections are used for permeation grouting and soil consolidation. The "other" market may include specialized applications such as slab lifting and concrete repair. Each type of polyurethane injection offers unique properties and benefits for different project requirements.</p></p>
