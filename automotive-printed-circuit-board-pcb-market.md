<p><p>Automotive Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Market consists of three main types - Double-Sided PCB, Multi-Layer PCB, and Single-Sided PCB. Double-Sided PCBs have circuits on both sides of the board, allowing for more complex designs. Multi-Layer PCBs have multiple layers of traces and connections, enabling greater functionality in a smaller space. Single-Sided PCBs have circuits on only one side of the board, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Each type caters to different automotive electronic system requirements, offering unique benefits and applications.</p></p>
