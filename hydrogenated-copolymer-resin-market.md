<p><ul><li>C5-C9</li><li>C9-DCPD</li><li>DCPD-C5</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrogenated copolymer resin market types include C5-C9, C9-DCPD, and DCPD-C5 resins. C5-C9 resins are produced from a mixture of C5 and C9 hydrocarbons, offering a balance between flexibility and tack. C9-DCPD resins are made from C9 aromatic hydrocarbons and dicyclopentadiene, providing excellent adhesion and heat resistance. DCPD-C5 resins combine properties of DCPD and C5 resins, resulting in a versatile adhesive with high tensile strength and temperature resistance. Each type caters to specific applications within the adhesive industry.</p></p>
