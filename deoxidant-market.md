Metallic deoxidizers, such as aluminum and ferrosilicon, are commonly used in steelmaking to remove oxygen from molten metal. Non-metallic deoxidizers, like lime and calcium carbide, are often used in industries such as electronics and chemical manufacturing to eliminate oxygen from various processes. Both types of deoxidants play a crucial role in improving product quality and reducing impurities in different industrial applications.</p></p>
