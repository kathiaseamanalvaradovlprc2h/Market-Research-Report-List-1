<p><p>Marble countertops come in two main types - artificial and natural. Natural marble, on the other hand, is mined from quarries and offers unique veining and color variations. Both types cater to different market segments, with artificial marble appealing to budget-conscious consumers and natural marble attracting those who appreciate its luxurious and one-of-a-kind aesthetic.</p></p>
