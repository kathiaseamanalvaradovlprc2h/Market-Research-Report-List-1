<p><strong>The Side Loader Refuse Trucks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Side Loading</li><li>Manual Side Loading</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Side loader refuse trucks are divided into two main market types: Automatic Side Loading (ASL) and Manual Side Loading (MSL). ASL trucks use an automated arm to lift and empty garbage containers into the truck, reducing the need for manual labor. This market type is popular in urban areas with high waste volumes. On the other hand, MSL trucks require workers to manually empty bins into the truck, making them more suitable for smaller communities or rural areas with lower waste volumes. Both options have their own advantages depending on the specific needs of the area.</p></p>
