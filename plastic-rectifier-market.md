<p><p>The plastic rectifier market consists of different types based on different specifications. Maximum reverse current refers to the highest current allowed in the reverse direction. Maximum peak current signifies the highest current allowed in the forward direction during peak loads. Maximum reverse voltage determines the highest voltage that can be applied in the reverse direction. Forward voltage is the voltage required for the rectifier to conduct properly. Maximum average rectified current represents the highest average current that the rectifier can handle over a given period.</p></p>
