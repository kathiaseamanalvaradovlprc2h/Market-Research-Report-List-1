<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin</li><li>Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pectins market is categorized into three major types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin, Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin, Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin. HM pectin is commonly used in food products like jams and jellies due to its gelling properties. LMC pectin is used in dairy and bakery products for stabilizing and texturizing. LMA pectin, with enhanced stability in low pH conditions, is utilized in acidic beverages and desserts. Each type serves specific purposes in the food industry based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
