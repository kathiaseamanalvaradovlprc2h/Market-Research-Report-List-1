<p><p>Ranging optical sensors are devices used to measure distances using light. There are two main types of ranging optical sensors in the market: laser and LED. Laser sensors utilize laser beams to measure distances with high precision and accuracy, making them ideal for long-range applications. On the other hand, LED sensors use light-emitting diodes to measure distances and are commonly used for shorter-range applications. Both types of sensors have their own strengths and are widely used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
