<p><p>Polycrystalline diamond (PCD) market consists of two main types - Polycrystalline Diamond Segments (PDSs) and Polycrystalline Diamond Compacts (PDCs). PDSs are widely used in cutting tools for machining non-ferrous metals and non-metallic materials. PDCs, on the other hand, are primarily utilized in drilling and mining applications due to their high wear resistance and durability. Both PDSs and PDCs are valued for their hardness and abrasion resistance, making them versatile materials in various industrial applications.</p></p>
