<p><strong>The Audio SoC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio SoC market can be divided into two major types: Analog and Digital. Analog audio SoCs process sound signals in their original form, without converting them into digital signals. They are typically used in applications where high-quality audio performance is critical. Digital audio SoCs, on the other hand, convert sound signals into digital data for processing, storage, and transmission. They are commonly used in mobile devices, headphones, and other consumer electronics products. Both types of audio SoCs play a crucial role in delivering high-quality audio experiences to users.</p></p>
