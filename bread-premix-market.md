<p><ul><li>Complete Mix</li><li>Dough-Base Mix</li><li>Dough Concentrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bread premix market can be categorized into three types: Complete Mix, Dough-Base Mix, and Dough Concentrates. Complete Mix includes all the necessary ingredients for making bread in one package, making it convenient for bakers. Dough-Base Mix only includes the basic dry ingredients, requiring bakers to add water and yeast. Dough Concentrates are highly concentrated mixes that require additional ingredients like flour and water. Each type offers varying levels of convenience and customization for bakers.</p></p>
