<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital temperature sensors come in two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact sensors require physical contact with the object being measured in order to accurately determine temperature, whereas non-contact sensors use techniques such as infrared radiation to measure temperature from a distance. Contact sensors are typically more accurate but may not be suitable for all applications, while non-contact sensors offer convenience and versatility in measuring temperature without needing direct contact with the object. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
