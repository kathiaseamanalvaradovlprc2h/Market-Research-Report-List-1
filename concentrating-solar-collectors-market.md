<p><p>Concentrating Solar Collectors market can be classified into various types such as Flat Plate Collector, Evacuated Tube Collector, Solar Air Collector, and other types. Flat Plate Collectors are made of a dark absorber plate enclosed in a glazed frame. Evacuated Tube Collectors utilize rows of parallel glass tubes containing absorber fins. Solar Air Collectors work by using sunlight to heat air which is then circulated through a building. Other types of collectors may include parabolic troughs, dish systems, and Fresnel reflectors.</p></p>
