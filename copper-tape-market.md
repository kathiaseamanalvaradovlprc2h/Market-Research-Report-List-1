<p><p>Copper tape market types are categorized based on different thicknesses. Below 0.1mm tapes are mainly used for flexible circuits and antennas due to their thinness. 0.1mm tapes find applications in EMI shielding and electrical components. Tapes in 0.1~0.5mm range are commonly used in transformers and electric motors. Those in 0.5~1mm range are suitable for power cables and busbars. Tapes above 1mm thickness are typically utilized in heat sinks and grounding applications due to their higher conductivity. Each market segment serves specific industry needs.</p></p>
