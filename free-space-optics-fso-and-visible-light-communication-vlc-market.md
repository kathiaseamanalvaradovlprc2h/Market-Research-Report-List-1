<p><p>Free Space Optics (FSO) and Visible Light Communication (VLC) market types include Modulators, Transmitters, Demodulators, Receivers, Encoders, and Decoders. </p><p>Modulators are used to convert electrical signals into optical signals, while transmitters send the modulated optical signals through free space. Demodulators then convert the received optical signals back into electrical signals. Receivers capture and process the optical signals for decoding. Encoders are used to convert data into a format suitable for transmission, while decoders convert received data back into its original form.</p></p>
