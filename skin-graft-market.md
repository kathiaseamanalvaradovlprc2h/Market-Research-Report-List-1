<p><strong>The Skin Graft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autologous</li><li>Isogeneic</li><li>Allogeneic</li><li>Xenogeneic</li><li>Prosthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin graft market is categorized into several types. Autologous grafts involve transplanting the patient's own skin from one area of the body to another. Isogeneic grafts involve transferring skin from an identical twin. Allogeneic grafts use skin from a donor who is genetically similar to the patient. Xenogeneic grafts use skin from a different species, often pigs. Prosthetic grafts involve using artificial materials to replace damaged or lost skin. Each type serves a specific purpose and has its own advantages and limitations.</p></p>
