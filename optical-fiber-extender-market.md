<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Extender Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Type</li><li>Multimode Fiber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Fiber Extenders can be classified into two main types based on the type of fiber used: Single Mode Fiber Type and Multimode Fiber Type. Single Mode Fiber Type extenders are designed for long-distance applications and have a smaller core size, allowing for higher bandwidth and lower attenuation. Multimode Fiber Type extenders are better suited for shorter distances and have a larger core size, making them more cost-effective for shorter runs. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the market for optical fiber extenders.</p></p>
