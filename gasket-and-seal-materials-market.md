<p><p>Gasket and seal materials market can be categorized into several types including rubber, fiber, silicone, graphite, PTFE, and other materials. Rubber gaskets are commonly used for their flexibility and durability. Fiber gaskets are preferred for their resistance to high temperatures and pressures. Silicone gaskets offer excellent sealing properties at extreme temperatures. Graphite gaskets are known for their high thermal conductivity. PTFE gaskets provide chemical resistance and low friction. Other materials may include cork, metal, and foam gaskets, each offering specific sealing properties for various applications.</p></p>
