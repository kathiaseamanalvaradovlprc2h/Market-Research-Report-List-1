<p><p>Shaving lotions and creams market can be categorized into two main types: aftershave and pre-shave products. Aftershave products are designed to soothe and moisturize the skin after shaving, helping to reduce any irritation or razor burns. These products often contain ingredients like aloe vera, witch hazel, or menthol, offering a refreshing and cooling sensation. On the other hand, pre-shave products are used before shaving to prepare the skin, soften the facial hair, and provide a smooth shaving experience. These include pre-shave oils or gels that help nourish and hydrate the skin, making it easier to shave off hair.</p></p>
