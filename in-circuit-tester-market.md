<p><p>An in-circuit tester is a specialized tool used to test the functionality of electronic components on a printed circuit board (PCB) while they are still assembled in the circuit. In-circuit testers can detect faults such as shorts, opens, incorrect components, and solder defects.</p><p>The in-circuit tester market is expected to show significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 14.5% during the forecast period.
