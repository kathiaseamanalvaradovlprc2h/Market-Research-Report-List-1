<p><strong>The MEMS Probe Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Probe Cards</li><li>Cantilever Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS probe cards are used in the semiconductor industry for testing semiconductor devices. Types of MEMS probe cards include vertical probe cards and cantilever probe cards. Vertical probe cards have vertically aligned probes for contacting the device under test, while cantilever probe cards have horizontally extending probes. Vertical probe cards are known for higher contact density and better performance, while cantilever probe cards are preferred for their flexibility in testing various device sizes and types. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
