<p><p>The wafer packaging materials market consists of various types including lead frames, package substrates, ceramic packaging materials, bonding wires, packaging materials, and die bonding materials. Lead frames are commonly used as a base structure for semiconductor devices. Package substrates provide electrical connections between the die and the outer environment. Ceramic packaging materials offer high thermal conductivity and reliability. Bonding wires are used for connecting the die to the package. Packaging materials provide protection for the semiconductor device. Die bonding materials are used for attaching the die to the package.</p></p>
