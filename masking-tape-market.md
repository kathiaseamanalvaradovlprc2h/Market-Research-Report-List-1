<p><p>Masking tape is available in various types based on the materials used in its production. Polyester-based masking tape is known for its durability and heat resistance. Polyimide-based tape is commonly used in high-temperature applications. Glass-cloth tape offers high tensile strength and is suitable for heavy-duty masking tasks. Vinyl tape is known for its flexibility and moisture resistance. Other types of masking tape may include paper-based or foam-based options, each with unique characteristics suited for different applications.</p></p>
