<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Monitor Display market consists of two main types: Windshield Projected Head-Up Display (HUD) and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD displays information directly onto the windshield, allowing drivers to see important data without taking their eyes off the road. Combiner Projected HUD projects information onto a small transparent screen that pops up in front of the driver's line of sight. Both types of HUDs enhance driver safety by providing essential information while minimizing distractions.</p></p>
