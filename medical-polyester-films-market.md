<p><p>Medical polyester films are classified into three main types based on their thickness: less than 100μm, 100-300μm, and more than 300μm. Films less than 100μm are often used for applications requiring flexibility and a lower level of barrier protection. Films in the range of 100-300μm offer a balance between flexibility and barrier properties. Films exceeding 300μm are typically used for applications where high barrier protection is essential. Each type of film caters to specific requirements in the medical industry.</p></p>
