<p><p>Amorphous Poly Alpha Olefin (APAO) market can be categorized into four main types based on the types of polymers used: Propylene Homopolymer, Copolymer of Propylene and Ethylene, Propylene Homopolymer and Ethylene Copolymer, and Other markets. Propylene Homopolymer refers to APAO derived solely from propylene, while Copolymer of Propylene and Ethylene involves a combination of propylene and ethylene. Propylene Homopolymer and Ethylene Copolymer blend both propylene homopolymer and ethylene in varying ratios. The Other market category encompasses APAO derived from different combinations of alpha olefins beyond propylene and ethylene.</p></p>
