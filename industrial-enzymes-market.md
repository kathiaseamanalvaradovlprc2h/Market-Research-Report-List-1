<p><p>Industrial enzymes market types include saccharifying enzymes, amylase, protease, lipases, and others. Saccharifying enzymes are used for converting starch into sugars, while amylase enzymes break down starch into simple sugars. Protease enzymes are utilized for protein breakdown, and lipases are used for fat hydrolysis. Other types of industrial enzymes include cellulase, phytase, and pectinase, which have various applications in the food and beverage, textile, and pharmaceutical industries. Each type of enzyme plays a crucial role in industrial processes by catalyzing specific reactions.</p></p>
