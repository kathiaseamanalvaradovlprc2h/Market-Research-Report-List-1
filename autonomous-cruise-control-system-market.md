<p><strong>The Autonomous Cruise Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Assisting systems</li><li>Multi-sensor systems</li><li>Predictive systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Assisting systems are designed to provide basic cruise control functions such as maintaining a set speed and keeping a safe distance from other vehicles. Multi-sensor systems utilize a combination of sensors like radar, lidar, and cameras to track surrounding vehicles, pedestrians, and road conditions for more accurate control. Predictive systems use advanced algorithms to anticipate traffic patterns and adjust speed accordingly. These three types of autonomous cruise control systems cater to different needs and offer varying levels of automation and safety features.</p></p>
