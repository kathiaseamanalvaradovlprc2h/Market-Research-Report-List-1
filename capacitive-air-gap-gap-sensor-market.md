<p><p>Capacitive air gap sensors are commonly used in industrial settings for measuring the distance between two objects. There are three main types of capacitive air gap sensors in the market: direct acting valve, pilot-operated valve, and others. Direct acting valve sensors operate by directly regulating the air gap between the objects. Pilot-operated valve sensors work by using a pilot valve to control the air gap. Other types of sensors may include variations on these designs or entirely different mechanisms for measuring air gaps.</p></p>
