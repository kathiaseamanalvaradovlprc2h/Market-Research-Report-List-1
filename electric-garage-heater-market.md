<p><strong>The Electric Garage Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-forced</li><li>Infrared</li><li>Ceramic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric garage heaters come in three main types - fan-forced, infrared, and ceramic. </p><p>Fan-forced heaters use a fan to distribute warm air evenly throughout the space, making them effective for larger areas. </p><p>Infrared heaters emit radiant heat that warms objects directly, making them efficient for spot heating in specific areas. </p><p>Ceramic heaters use ceramic heating elements to provide consistent heat and maintain a comfortable temperature in the garage. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different heating needs in a garage setting.</p></p>
