<p><p>The Railway Hydraulic Damper market includes primary and secondary suspension dampers, which are designed to absorb and control the oscillations and vibrations of railway vehicles. Primary suspension dampers are used in the vertical movement of the vehicle, while secondary suspension dampers are used in the horizontal movement. Additionally, yaw dampers are used to control the yawing motion of the railway vehicle to ensure stability and safety during operation. These dampers play a crucial role in improving ride comfort and reducing wear and tear on the vehicle components.</p></p>
