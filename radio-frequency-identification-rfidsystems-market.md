<p><ul><li>Passive RFID System</li><li>Active RFID System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID systems are used for tracking and identifying objects using radio waves. Passive RFID systems do not have a power source and rely on the reader's signal to transmit information, making them cost-effective for tracking items in close range. Active RFID systems, on the other hand, have their own power source, allowing for longer distance tracking and real-time monitoring of assets. Both systems have their unique advantages and are used in various industries such as logistics, healthcare, and retail for tracking inventory and assets.</p></p>
