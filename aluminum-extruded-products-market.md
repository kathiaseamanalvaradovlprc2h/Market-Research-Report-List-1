<p><p>Aluminum extruded products are available in three main market types: mill-finished, powder-coated, and anodized. Mill-finished products have a natural shiny appearance due to the extrusion process. Powder-coated products are coated with a durable finish applied as a free-flowing, dry powder. Anodized products have a protective oxide layer applied electrolytically to enhance durability and corrosion resistance. Each market type offers specific benefits in terms of aesthetics, durability, and customization options for various applications in the construction, automotive, and aerospace industries.</p></p>
