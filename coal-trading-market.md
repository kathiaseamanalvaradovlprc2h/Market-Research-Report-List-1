Lignite is the lowest quality, with high moisture content and low heat capacity. Sub-bituminous has slightly higher heat capacity and lower moisture content. Bituminous is a widely traded type with moderate heat capacity and lower moisture content. Anthracite is the highest quality, with the highest heat capacity and lowest moisture content, making it the most sought-after type in the market.</p></p>
