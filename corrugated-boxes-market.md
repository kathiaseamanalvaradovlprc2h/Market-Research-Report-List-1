<p><p>Corrugated boxes are classified into three main types based on the number of layers of corrugated medium within them: single, double, and triple corrugated. Single corrugated boxes have one layer of fluted paper sandwiched between two layers of linerboard. Double corrugated boxes have two layers of fluted paper sandwiched between three layers of linerboard. Triple corrugated boxes have three layers of fluted paper sandwiched between four layers of linerboard. Each type offers varying levels of strength and durability, making them suitable for different packaging needs.</p></p>
