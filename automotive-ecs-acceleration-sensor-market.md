<p><p>Automotive ECS (Electronic Control System) acceleration sensors come in three main types: single, double, and triple. </p><p>Single acceleration sensors provide data in one direction, typically measuring acceleration in a straight line. Double acceleration sensors can measure acceleration in two directions simultaneously, while triple acceleration sensors are capable of measuring acceleration in three directions, offering a comprehensive view of the vehicle's movements. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and applications, depending on the specific needs of the ECS system in the automotive industry.</p></p>
