<p><p>Wood vinegar, also known as pyroligneous acid, is produced through the process of pyrolysis. There are three main types of wood vinegar market based on the pyrolysis process: Slow Pyrolysis, Fast Pyrolysis, and Intermediate Pyrolysis. Slow pyrolysis involves heating wood at a slow rate to produce higher quality wood vinegar. Fast pyrolysis involves heating wood at a rapid rate to produce larger quantities of wood vinegar. Intermediate pyrolysis falls in between slow and fast pyrolysis in terms of heating rate and wood vinegar quality.</p></p>
