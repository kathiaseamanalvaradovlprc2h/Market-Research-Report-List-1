<p><p>Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators are devices used to support breathing in patients with respiratory conditions. In the home use market, there are two main types of NPVs: Single Level Ventilators and Bilevel Ventilators. Single Level Ventilators provide a fixed pressure throughout the breathing cycle, while Bilevel Ventilators offer different pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation. Both types are commonly used in home settings for patients who require respiratory assistance but do not need invasive ventilation.</p></p>
