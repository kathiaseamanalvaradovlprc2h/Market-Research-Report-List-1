<p><p>Aircraft transparencies market can be categorized into four main types based on the substrate material used: Glass Substrate, Acrylic Substrate, Polycarbonate Substrate, and Others. Glass substrates are known for their high optical clarity and scratch resistance. Acrylic substrates offer lightweight and impact resistance properties. Polycarbonate substrates are valued for their exceptional toughness and flexibility. The "Others" category includes transparencies made from unique materials such as laminated glass or coated plastics. Each substrate type serves different applications within the aircraft industry.</p></p>
