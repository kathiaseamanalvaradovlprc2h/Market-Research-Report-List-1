<p><p>UHF Inlays, also known as Ultra High Frequency Inlays, are passive RFID tags that operate at the UHF frequency range of 860-960 MHz. They are commonly used in applications such as inventory management, asset tracking, and supply chain management. UHF Inlays consist of an antenna and a small microchip that stores information which can be read wirelessly by RFID readers.</p><p>The UHF Inlays Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to increasing adoption of RFID technology in industries such as retail, healthcare, and transportation for improved efficiency and visibility.
