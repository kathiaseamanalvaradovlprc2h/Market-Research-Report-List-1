<p><p>Hall Effect linear position sensors come in two main types: non-contact and contact position sensors. Non-contact sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect the position of a target without physical contact, offering benefits such as high precision and durability. On the other hand, contact position sensors rely on physical contact with the target to determine its position, providing a more cost-effective solution for some applications. Both types of sensors offer unique advantages and are used in a variety of industries for position sensing applications.</p></p>
