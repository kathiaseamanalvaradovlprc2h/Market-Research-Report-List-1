<p><p>HVAC relay market offers various types of relays to control heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. Electromagnetic relays use an electromagnet to operate the switch, while solid-state relays use semiconductors to switch the current. Thermal relays protect systems from overheating by interrupting the circuit when temperatures exceed a set limit. Hybrid relays combine features of different types to optimize performance. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different HVAC applications based on their specific characteristics and requirements.</p></p>
