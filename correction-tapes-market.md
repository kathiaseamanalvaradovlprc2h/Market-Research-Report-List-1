<p><p>Correction tapes are available in two main market types: disposable and replaceable. Disposable correction tapes are single-use products that are discarded after all the tape has been used up. On the other hand, replaceable correction tapes have refillable cartridges that can be easily replaced once the tape runs out, making them a more sustainable and cost-effective option. Both types of correction tapes serve the same purpose of covering mistakes on paper, but they differ in terms of convenience and environmental impact.</p></p>
