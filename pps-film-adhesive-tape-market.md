<p><p>PPS film adhesive tapes come in two main types - silicone based and rubber-based. Silicone-based tapes are known for their high-temperature resistance and excellent chemical resistance, making them ideal for applications in extreme conditions. On the other hand, rubber-based tapes are more cost-effective, offer good adhesion to a variety of surfaces, and are commonly used in general-purpose applications. Both types of tapes offer strong bonding properties and durability, catering to a wide range of industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
