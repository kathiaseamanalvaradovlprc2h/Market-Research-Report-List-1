<p><p>Flexible Printed Circuit Board (FPCB) market consists of various types including single sided flex circuits, double sided flex circuits, multi-layer flex circuits, rigid flex circuits, and others. Single sided flex circuits have conductive layer on one side, while double sided flex circuits have conductive layers on both sides. Multi-layer flex circuits have three or more conductive layers, providing flexibility and space savings. Rigid flex circuits combine rigid and flexible substrates for increased durability. Other types of FPCBs include sculptured, double access, and dual layer coverlay flex circuits.</p></p>
