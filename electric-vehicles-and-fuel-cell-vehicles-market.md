<p><p>Electric Vehicles (EV) market can be categorized into Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV). BEVs run solely on electricity stored in a battery, while PHEVs have both an electric motor and a fuel-powered engine, offering flexibility by being able to run on electricity or gasoline. Both types of vehicles contribute to reducing emissions and dependency on fossil fuels.</p></p>
