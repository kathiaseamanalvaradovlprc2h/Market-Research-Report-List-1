<p><p>Virgin containerboard is commonly available in two main market types: linerboard and corrugating medium. Linerboard is a high-strength paper used for the outer layer of corrugated boxes, providing protection and durability. It is often made from virgin fibers for enhanced strength. Corrugating medium, on the other hand, is the fluted layer that provides cushioning and support in between the layers of linerboard. Both linerboard and corrugating medium play crucial roles in the strength and performance of corrugated packaging materials.</p></p>
