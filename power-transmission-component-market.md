<p><p>Power transmission components refer to various equipment used in the transmission and distribution of electricity. The market includes transformers, circuit breakers, insulators, arrestors, transmission lines, transmission towers, and other related components. Transformers are used to increase or decrease voltage levels, while circuit breakers protect the system from overloads. Insulators prevent electricity from escaping transmission lines, and arrestors protect equipment from voltage surges. Transmission towers support the transmission lines, while other components include switches, conductors, and capacitors used in the power grid.</p></p>
