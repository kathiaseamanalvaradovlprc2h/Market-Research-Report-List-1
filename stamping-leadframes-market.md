<p><p>Stamping leadframes are commonly used in the semiconductor industry for packaging integrated circuits. There are various types of leadframes such as SOP (Small Outline Package), SIP (Single In-line Package), DIP (Dual In-line Package), QFN (Quad Flat No-Lead), QFP (Quad Flat Package), SOIC (Small Outline Integrated Circuit), and others. Each type has its own unique design and configuration to accommodate different requirements for IC packaging. These leadframes play a crucial role in providing electrical connections between the IC chip and the outer circuit.</p></p>
