<p><strong>The Application Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Custom Design ASIC</li><li>Semi-custom Design ASIC (Standard Cell Based ASIC and Gate Array Based ASIC)</li><li>Programmable ASIC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ASIC market can be categorized into three types based on design complexity and customization options. Full Custom Design ASICs offer maximum performance and customization but at a higher cost. Semi-custom Design ASICs includes Standard Cell Based ASIC and Gate Array Based ASIC, providing a balance between performance and cost. Programmable ASICs allow for flexibility and reprogramming but may have lower performance compared to full custom or semi-custom designs. These different types cater to varying needs and budgets in the ASIC market.</p></p>
