<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electron Microscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs)</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electron microscopes are advanced instruments used for high-resolution imaging of extremely small objects. The market for electron microscopes is categorized into two main types: Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEMs) and Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEMs). SEMs use a focused beam of electrons to scan the surface of a sample, generating detailed 3D images. TEMs, on the other hand, transmit an electron beam through a thin sample, creating high-resolution images of the internal structure. Both SEMs and TEMs have wide applications in scientific research, material science, biology, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
