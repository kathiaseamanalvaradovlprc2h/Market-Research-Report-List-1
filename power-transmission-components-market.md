<p><p>Power transmission components market includes transformers, circuit breakers, isolators, insulators, arrestors, transmission lines, and transmission towers. Transformers are used to change voltage levels, while circuit breakers protect the system from overloads. Isolators are used for isolating circuits for maintenance. Insulators prevent electricity leakage. Arrestors protect equipment from lightning strikes. Transmission lines carry electricity over long distances. Transmission towers support transmission lines. These components play a crucial role in the efficient and safe transmission of power.</p></p>
