<p><p>Frequency counters are available in various types to cater to different needs. Amp-Clamp adapters are used for measuring current in electrical systems. Dual counters can simultaneously measure two different frequencies. Embedded frequency counters are integrated into other electronic devices for convenience. Handheld counters are portable and easy to use on-the-go. Rate counters measure the frequency of events over time. Totalizer counters provide a cumulative total count of events. Each type of frequency counter caters to specific requirements in various industries and applications.</p></p>
