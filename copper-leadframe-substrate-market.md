<p><p>Copper leadframe substrates are commonly used in semiconductor packaging processes. There are two main types of lead frame manufacturing processes: Stamping process lead frames and etching process lead frames. Stamping process lead frames involve cutting and shaping copper sheets into the desired design, while etching process lead frames use a chemical process to selectively remove material from a copper sheet to create the lead frame design. Both processes are widely used in the semiconductor industry for packaging integrated circuits.</p></p>
