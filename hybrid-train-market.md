Battery-operated trains use rechargeable batteries, and LNG trains run on liquefied natural gas. The "others" category may include alternative fuel sources such as hydrogen or biofuels. Each type of hybrid train offers unique benefits in terms of efficiency, emissions reduction, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
