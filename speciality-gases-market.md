Pure gases are single-component gases that have high purity levels and are used for specific applications where impurities can affect the desired outcome. Gas mixtures, on the other hand, are combinations of two or more gases in specific ratios, tailored to meet the requirements of unique industrial processes. These gases are produced to meet precise specifications and are crucial in various industries such as healthcare, electronics, and aerospace.</p></p>
