<p><p>The Light Sport Aircraft (LSA) market includes three main types: Special Light Sport Aircraft (S-LSA), Experimental Light Sport Aircraft (E-LSA), and others. S-LSA are factory-built aircraft that meet specific design and performance criteria set by aviation authorities. E-LSA are kits that can be assembled by individuals, but must adhere to the same design standards as S-LSA. The "others" category includes aircraft that do not fit into the S-LSA or E-LSA definitions, such as some ultralight aircraft.</p></p>
