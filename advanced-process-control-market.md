<p><p>Advanced Process Control refers to technology used in various industries to optimize and control production processes. Market types within this sector include Advanced Regulatory Control, which focuses on maintaining process variables within set limits. Multivariable Model Predictive Control utilizes mathematical models to predict and control multiple variables simultaneously. Inferential Control involves making decisions based on indirect measurements. Sequential Control coordinates the execution of multiple processes in a specific order. Compressor Control specifically targets the efficient operation of compressors in industrial settings.</p></p>
