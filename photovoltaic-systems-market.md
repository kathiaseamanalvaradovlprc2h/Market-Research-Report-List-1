<p><p>Photovoltaic systems market can be categorized into three main types based on the material used for solar panel production. Single Crystal Silicon panels are made from high-purity silicon material, offering high efficiency but also higher cost. Polycrystalline Silicon panels are made from silicon fragments melted together, providing a cost-effective alternative to single crystal panels. Other types in the market include thin-film, amorphous silicon, and cadmium telluride panels, each offering unique advantages and applications in the solar energy industry.</p></p>
