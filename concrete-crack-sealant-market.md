<p><p>Concrete crack sealant market consists mainly of two types: acrylic and polyurethane. Acrylic sealants are water-based, easy to apply, and offer good flexibility. They are suitable for minor cracks and gaps. On the other hand, polyurethane sealants are known for their high durability and resistance to wear and tear. They are ideal for larger and deeper cracks. Both types of sealants are popular choices for repairing and sealing cracks in concrete structures, offering different benefits based on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
