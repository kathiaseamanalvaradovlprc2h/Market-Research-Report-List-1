<p><strong>The Light-curing Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light-curing adhesives are classified into two main types: Water-based adhesives and Solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives use water as a carrier medium, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-based adhesives, on the other hand, use solvents as a carrier medium for the adhesive components. They offer high bonding strength but may emit volatile organic compounds. Both types of adhesives are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and medical devices for their fast curing time and high performance.</p></p>
