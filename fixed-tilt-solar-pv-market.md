<p><p>Fixed tilt solar PV market consists of three main types of solar panels - Mono-Si, Multi-Si, and Thin Film. Mono-Si panels are made from single-crystal silicon, offering higher efficiency and longevity. Multi-Si panels are made from multiple silicon crystals, providing a balance between efficiency and cost. Thin Film panels are made from various materials like cadmium telluride or amorphous silicon, offering flexibility and a lower cost. Each type caters to different needs and budgets in the solar PV market.</p></p>
