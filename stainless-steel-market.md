<p><p>Stainless Steel Market Types comprise Ferritic Stainless Steel, known for its corrosion resistance and high strength. PH Stainless Steel offers high strength and toughness at low temperatures. Duplex Stainless Steel combines the benefits of austenitic and ferritic grades.
