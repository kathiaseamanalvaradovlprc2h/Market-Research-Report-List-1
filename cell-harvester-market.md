<p><strong>The Cell Harvester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement collector</li><li>Therapy collector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell harvester market can be classified into two main types: displacement collector and therapy collector. Displacement collectors are used for harvesting cells by displacing them from tissues through mechanical or enzymatic methods. Therapy collectors, on the other hand, are employed for collecting cells that can be used for therapeutic purposes, such as stem cells for regenerative medicine. Both types of cell harvesters play a crucial role in various fields like research, diagnostics, and treatment, driving the market growth in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
