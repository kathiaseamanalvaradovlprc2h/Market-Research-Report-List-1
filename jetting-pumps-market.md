<p><strong>The Jetting Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deep Well</li><li>Shallow Well</li><li>Convertible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jetting pumps are categorized into three main types based on their application in water supply systems: deep well, shallow well, and convertible pumps. Deep well jetting pumps are designed for use in deeper wells where water needs to be lifted from significant depths. Shallow well jetting pumps are suitable for shallower wells where water can be easily accessed. Convertible jetting pumps can be adjusted for use in both deep and shallow wells, offering flexibility in various water supply applications. Each type serves a specific purpose based on the well depth and water requirements.</p></p>
