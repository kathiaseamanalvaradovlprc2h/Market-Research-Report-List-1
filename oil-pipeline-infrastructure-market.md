<p><ul><li>Crude Oil</li><li>Petroleum Product</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil pipeline infrastructure market consists of two main types: crude oil pipelines and petroleum product pipelines. Crude oil pipelines are used to transport raw, unrefined oil from production sites to refineries for processing. Petroleum product pipelines, on the other hand, are used to transport processed oil products, such as gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel, from refineries to distribution centers and storage facilities. Both types of pipelines play a crucial role in ensuring a steady and efficient flow of oil and oil products throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
