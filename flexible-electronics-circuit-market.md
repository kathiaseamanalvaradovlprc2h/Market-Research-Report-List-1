<p><p>Flexible electronics circuit market can be classified into three main types based on their structure - Single-Sided, Multilayer, and Rigid circuits. Single-Sided circuits consist of a single conductive layer, making them lightweight and bendable. Multilayer circuits are made up of multiple layers of conductive material separated by insulating layers, providing higher functionality and efficiency. Rigid circuits are traditional circuits made on solid substrates, offering stability and durability. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the growing flexible electronics market.</p></p>
