<p><p>Automotive solenoid valves market offers various types including 2-Way, 3-Way, 4-Way, 5-Way valves, and Others. 2-Way valves have one inlet and one outlet port for on-off control. 3-Way valves have three ports for flow control by redirecting fluid between different paths. 4-Way valves are commonly used for directional control of double acting cylinders. 5-Way valves distribute fluid among multiple cylinders or control multiple circuits. The "Others" category may include additional valve configurations catering to specific applications.</p></p>
