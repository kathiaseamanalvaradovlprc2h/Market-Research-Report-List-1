<p><p>Textile colorants are available in various forms such as powder, granules, paste, and liquid in the market. Powder colorants are dry and easily soluble in water, while granules are small solid particles that can be dissolved or dispersed. Paste colorants are thick and viscous, making them suitable for printing applications. Liquid colorants are already in a liquid state and can be easily mixed with water or other solvents. Each type of colorant offers different characteristics and benefits for dyeing and coloring textiles.</p></p>
