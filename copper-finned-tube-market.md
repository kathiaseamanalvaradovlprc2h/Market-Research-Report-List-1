<p><ul><li>Single Metal Finned Tubes</li><li>Bimetal Composite Finned Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper Finned Tube market offers two main types of products: Single Metal Finned Tubes and Bimetal Composite Finned Tubes. Single Metal Finned Tubes are made entirely of copper and are known for their high thermal conductivity and corrosion resistance. On the other hand, Bimetal Composite Finned Tubes combine copper with another metal, such as aluminum or stainless steel, to enhance heat transfer performance and durability. Both types of finned tubes are commonly used in applications requiring efficient heat exchange, such as in HVAC systems and industrial processes.</p></p>
