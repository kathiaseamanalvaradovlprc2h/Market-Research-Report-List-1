<p><p>Heat resistance glass market consists of three main types: tempered glass, Pyrex glass, and Pyroceram glass. Tempered glass is strengthened through a heating and rapid cooling process, making it more durable and heat-resistant. Pyrex glass is known for its ability to withstand extreme temperatures and sudden changes without shattering. Pyroceram glass is a type of ceramic glass that is highly resistant to thermal shock and can be used in high-temperature applications. Each of these types serves different industries and applications within the heat resistance glass market.</p></p>
