<p><p>Noncontact temperature measurement market includes two types: infrared temperature measurement and optical fiber temperature measurement. Infrared temperature measurement utilizes infrared radiation to measure an object's temperature without physical contact. This method is widely used in industries like manufacturing and healthcare. Optical fiber temperature measurement, on the other hand, uses optical fibers to measure temperature by detecting changes in refractive index or luminescence. This method is commonly used in high-temperature environments where traditional methods may not be feasible.</p></p>
