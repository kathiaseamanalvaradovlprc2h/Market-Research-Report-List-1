<p><p>Nickel targets can be classified into two main types based on their design and function: plane targets and rotating targets. On the other hand, rotating targets are constantly moving, providing a more efficient and even deposition of nickel onto the desired substrate. Each type of target serves a specific purpose in various industries where nickel plating is required, catering to different production needs and preferences.</p></p>
