<p><p>Glyphosate herbicide market consists of two main types: selective herbicide and non-selective herbicide. Selective herbicides target specific types of weeds while leaving desired plants unharmed, making them suitable for use in agriculture and landscaping. Non-selective herbicides, on the other hand, kill all vegetation they come into contact with, making them useful for clearing large areas of land or controlling invasive species. Both types of herbicides play important roles in the management of weeds and vegetation in various settings.</p></p>
