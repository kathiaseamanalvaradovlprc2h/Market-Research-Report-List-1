<p><p>Glass fabrics market is segmented into four types based on weaving patterns: Plain Weave, Leno Weave, Twill Weave, and Satin Weave. Plain weave offers uniform strength and durability. Leno weave creates an open, breathable fabric suitable for filtration applications. Twill weave provides a diagonal pattern, enhancing flexibility and drape. Satin weave yields a smooth, glossy surface with high tensile strength. Each type caters to different needs in industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
