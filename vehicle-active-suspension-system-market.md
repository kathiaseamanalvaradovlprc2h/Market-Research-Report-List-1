<p><p>Vehicle Active Suspension System market types include Hydraulic Actuators, Pneumatic Actuators, and Electromagnetic Actuators. Hydraulic actuators utilize fluid power to control suspension movement, providing precise adjustments. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to regulate suspension dynamics, offering quick response times. Electromagnetic actuators use electromagnetic fields to adjust suspension components, offering high precision and control. Each type of actuator has its own unique advantages and applications in the vehicle active suspension system market.</p></p>
