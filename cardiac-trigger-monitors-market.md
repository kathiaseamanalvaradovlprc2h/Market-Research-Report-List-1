<p><p>Cardiac trigger monitors are devices used during medical procedures to detect the cardiac cycle and trigger the appropriate timing for interventions such as defibrillation. There are three main types of cardiac trigger monitors based on different methods of detection - ECG method which measures electrical activity of the heart, arterial pressure method which monitors changes in blood pressure, and photoelectric method which uses light sensors to detect blood flow. Each method offers unique advantages and applications in various medical settings.</p></p>
