<p><p>Carbon-ceramic discs are known for their high-performance and durability. There are two main types of carbon-ceramic discs on the market: drilled and slotted. Drilled discs have holes drilled into the surface to improve heat dissipation and reduce brake fade. Slotted discs have grooves or slots that help to evacuate gas and dust, resulting in better brake performance. Both types offer improved braking capabilities compared to traditional brake discs, making them popular choices for high-performance vehicles and racing applications.</p></p>
