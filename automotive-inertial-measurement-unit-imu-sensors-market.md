<p><strong>The Automotive Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs</li><li>FOG-based IMUs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs are compact and cost-effective sensors that use microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) technology to measure angular velocity and orientation in vehicles. They are widely used in automotive applications for stability control, collision avoidance, and navigation systems. On the other hand, Fiber Optic Gyroscope (FOG)-based IMUs offer higher accuracy and reliability compared to MEMS-based sensors but are more expensive. They are typically used in high-end automotive applications where precision is critical, such as autonomous driving and advanced driver assistance systems.</p></p>
