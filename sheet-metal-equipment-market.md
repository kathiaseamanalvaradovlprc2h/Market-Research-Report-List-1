<p><p>The sheet metal equipment market can be categorized into various types. Power press is a machine used to punch or shape metal sheets. Bending machines are employed to bend metal sheets into desired shapes. Hydraulic shearing machines are used to cut metal sheets using hydraulic power. These machines cater to the diverse needs of the sheet metal industry.</p></p>
