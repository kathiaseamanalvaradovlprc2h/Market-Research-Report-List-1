<p><p>Canes and crutches are mobility aids used by individuals who have difficulty walking. Canes come in various types such as folding canes, quad canes, and offset canes. Folding canes are convenient for travel, while quad canes provide extra stability. Offset canes are designed to reduce strain on the wrist. Crutches, on the other hand, come in types including axillary crutches and forearm crutches. Axillary crutches are placed under the armpits, while forearm crutches have cuffs that go around the forearm for support.</p></p>
