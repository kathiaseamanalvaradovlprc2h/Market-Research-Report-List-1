<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Thermal Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Open Thermal Switch</li><li>Normally Closed Thermal Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal switches are devices that open or close electrical circuits at specific temperatures to prevent overheating. Normally Open Thermal Switches are designed to remain open until reaching a set temperature, allowing electricity to flow once that temperature is reached. Normally Closed Thermal Switches, on the other hand, are designed to stay closed until reaching a set temperature, cutting off electricity flow once that temperature is reached. Both types of thermal switches play a crucial role in maintaining safe operating temperatures in various electrical and electronic devices.</p></p>
