<p><strong>The Plastic Bait Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard Bait</li><li>Soft Bait</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic bait market is segmented into two main types - hard bait and soft bait. Hard baits are typically made of durable, solid plastic material and are designed to mimic the appearance and movement of real baitfish or insects. They are ideal for aggressive fish species and fishing in heavy cover. On the other hand, soft baits are made of flexible plastic material that gives them a lifelike feel and action in the water. They are popular for targeting finicky or slow-moving fish species.</p></p>
