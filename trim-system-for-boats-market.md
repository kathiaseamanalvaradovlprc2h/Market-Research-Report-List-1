<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trim system for boats market consists of two main types: trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are small, adjustable plates attached to the stern of a boat that help stabilize the boat and improve performance by adjusting the angle of the hull. Interceptors are adjustable fins typically installed on the transom of a boat that work similarly to trim tabs in adjusting the boat's trim and angle in the water. Both trim tabs and interceptors are essential components for optimizing a boat's performance and handling.</p></p>
