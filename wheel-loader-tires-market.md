<p><p>Wheel loader tires are available in two main types - pneumatic tires and solid tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled and provide a smooth ride, good traction, and cushioning for shock absorption. They are ideal for use on uneven or rough terrains. Solid tires, on the other hand, are made of solid rubber and are puncture-proof, making them durable and maintenance-free. They are suitable for applications where flats and punctures are common, offering stability and reliability during operation.</p></p>
