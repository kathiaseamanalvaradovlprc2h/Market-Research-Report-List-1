<p><strong>The HD Digital Video Servers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>M-JPEG Technology</li><li>MPEG-4 Technology</li><li>H.264 Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HD Digital Video Servers Market Types are segmented based on the technology used for video compression and storage. M-JPEG Technology utilizes frame-by-frame compression, making it suitable for high-quality video but requiring more storage space. MPEG-4 Technology offers efficient compression while maintaining good video quality. H.264 Technology, also known as AVC, provides high-quality video at lower bit rates, reducing the amount of storage needed. Each technology caters to different needs and preferences in the market for digital video servers.</p></p>
