<p><ul><li>Polymerized PE Wax</li><li>Modified PE Wax</li><li>Cracked PE Wax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) wax is available in three main types: polymerized PE wax, modified PE wax, and cracked PE wax. Polymerized PE wax is produced by polymerization of ethylene and has excellent performance in terms of hardness and thermal stability. Modified PE wax is produced by adding various additives to enhance properties such as flexibility and adhesion. Cracked PE wax is produced through the cracking of PE molecules, resulting in lower molecular weight and improved compatibility with other materials. Each type of PE wax caters to different industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
