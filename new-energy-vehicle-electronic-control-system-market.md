<p><p>New Energy Vehicle Electronic Control System Market consists of two main types - Main Controller and Auxiliary Controller. The Main Controller is responsible for the overall operation and management of the vehicle's electronic systems, such as the battery management system and power distribution. On the other hand, the Auxiliary Controller is designed to support the main controller by handling specific functions, such as the heating and cooling systems or interior lighting. Both controllers work together to ensure optimal performance and efficiency of the vehicle's electronic control system.</p></p>
