<p><p>High Speed Direct Attach Copper (DAC) Cable market includes two main types: Passive DAC Cables and Active DAC Cables. Passive DAC Cables do not require power and are used for short-reach connections within data centers. Active DAC Cables, on the other hand, incorporate signal processing electronics to enable longer reach and higher data rates. Both types offer cost-effective solutions for high-speed data transmission, with Passive DAC Cables being more suitable for short distances and Active DAC Cables for longer distances.</p></p>
