<p><p>Yogurt starter markets can be categorized into three main types: liquid yogurt starter, frozen yogurt starter, and direct throw yogurt starter. Liquid yogurt starter is a concentrated form of live bacteria that can be easily mixed into milk to make yogurt. Frozen yogurt starter is typically freeze-dried and needs to be rehydrated before use. Direct throw yogurt starter is a ready-to-use product that can be directly added to milk without any additional preparation. Each type offers convenience and different levels of ease of use for making yogurt.</p></p>
