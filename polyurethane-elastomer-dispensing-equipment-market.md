<p><strong>The Polyurethane Elastomer Dispensing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Mixing</li><li>Filled Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane elastomer dispensing equipment market can be segmented into two types: dynamic mixing and filled systems. Dynamic mixing systems mix the two components of polyurethane elastomers (polyol and isocyanate) through a dynamic mixing process, while filled systems involve adding fillers or additives to the mixture. Dynamic mixing systems provide more control over the mixing process and offer higher flexibility in adjusting material properties, while filled systems are preferred for applications requiring specific properties such as increased hardness or thermal conductivity.</p></p>
