<p><p>The rear axle commodity market consists of three main types: lift axle, dead axle, and drive axle. Lift axles are inactive axles that can be raised or lowered to adjust the weight distribution of the vehicle. Dead axles are fixed axles that do not provide power to the wheels. Drive axles are active axles that transfer power from the engine to the wheels, allowing the vehicle to move.
