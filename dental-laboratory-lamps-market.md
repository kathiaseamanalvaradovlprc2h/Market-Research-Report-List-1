<p><p>The dental laboratory lamps market encompasses three main types: LED, fluorescent, and infrared lamps. LED lamps are preferred for their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and brightness control features. Fluorescent lamps produce light through the excitation of phosphor coating and are known for their affordability and color rendering capabilities. Infrared lamps emit infrared radiation, which helps in curing dental materials efficiently. Each type of lamp has its own advantages and is used in dental laboratories for various applications such as dental restoration, aesthetics, and examination purposes.</p></p>
