<p><p>A Combat Management System (CMS) is a software-defined solution that helps in integrating sensors, weapons, and information onto a single platform for efficient decision making in combat scenarios. These technologies help in improving the decision-making process and enable autonomous operations in combat scenarios. Furthermore, the integration of unmanned systems with CMS is gaining traction, ensuring effective coordination between manned and unmanned platforms.</p><p>The increasing adoption of CMS in naval applications is another trend boosting market growth.
