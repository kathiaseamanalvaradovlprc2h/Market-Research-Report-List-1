<p><p>Line Reactors are electrical components used to protect AC and DC motor drives from power system disturbances and harmonic distortions. AC Line Reactors are used in AC motor drives to reduce harmonics, protect the drive from voltage spikes, and prevent damage to the motor. Both types of Line Reactors are essential in ensuring the smooth operation and longevity of motor drives in industrial applications.</p></p>
