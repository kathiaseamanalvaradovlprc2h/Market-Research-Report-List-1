<p><p>Automotive seat belt pretensioners are safety devices that tighten the seat belts in a vehicle during sudden deceleration or impact. Mechanical pretensioners function through a mechanical system that immediately retracts the seat belt when triggered. Electrical pretensioners, on the other hand, use an electrical mechanism to retract the seat belt. Both types enhance passenger safety by reducing seat belt slack, minimizing the risk of occupants being thrown forward in an accident. These different types cater to various automotive manufacturers' preferences and requirements for seat belt safety systems.</p></p>
