<p><p>Sports watches can be categorized into two main types: mechanical watches and electronic watches. Mechanical watches rely on complex mechanisms and gears to function, requiring regular winding or movement to keep time accurately. On the other hand, electronic watches utilize battery-powered quartz movements to keep time, often incorporating additional features such as GPS, heart rate monitors, and fitness tracking. Both types cater to different preferences, with mechanical watches appealing to traditionalists and those who appreciate craftsmanship, while electronic watches appeal to athletes and tech-savvy individuals.</p></p>
