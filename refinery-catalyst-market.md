<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Refinery Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FCC Catalysts</li><li>Hydro-processing Catalyst</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refinery catalyst market can be categorized into three main types: FCC catalysts, hydro-processing catalysts, and others. FCC catalysts are used in fluid catalytic cracking units to convert heavy oils into lighter products. Hydro-processing catalysts are utilized in processes like hydrotreating and hydrocracking to remove impurities and enhance the quality of refined products. The "others" category may include catalysts used in various refining processes such as reforming, alkylation, and isomerization. Each type of catalyst plays a crucial role in improving the efficiency and effectiveness of refinery operations.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Refinery Catalyst Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersed Catalyst</li><li>Supported Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refinery catalysts are used in the petroleum refining process to enhance the efficiency of chemical reactions that occur during the refining process. Dispersed catalysts are finely divided particles that are suspended in the reaction mixture, providing a large surface area for reaction to take place. On the other hand, supported catalysts are anchored onto a support material, such as a porous solid, to facilitate the reaction. Both types of catalysts play a crucial role in improving the overall performance and yield of the refining process.</p></p>
